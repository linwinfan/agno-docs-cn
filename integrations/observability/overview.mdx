Observability helps us understand, debug, and improve AI 智能体s. Agno 支持 observability through [OpenTelemetry](https://opentelemetry.io/), integrating seamlessly with popular tracing and monitoring 平台s.

## Key Benefits
- **Trace**: Visualize and analyze 智能体 execution flows.
- **Monitor**: Track performance, errors, and usage.
- **Debug**: Quickly identify and resolve issues.

## OpenTelemetry Support

Agno offers first-class support for OpenTelemetry, the industry standard for 分布式 tracing and observability.
- **Auto-Instrumentation**: Automatically instrument your 智能体s and tools.
- **Flexible Export**: Send traces to any OpenTelemetry-兼容 backend.
- **Custom Tracing**: Extend or customize tracing as needed.

<Note>
OpenTelemetry-兼容 backends including Arize Phoenix, Langfuse, Langsmith, Langtrace, Logfire, Maxim, OpenLIT, Traceloop, and Weave are supported by Agno out of the box.
</Note>
